About

My name is Layla Flight, I'm 17 years old. One of my passions is mentoring. In school I am involved in this program called Peer Group Connections, where juniors and seniors help new coming freshman smoothly transition from middle school to high school. They work through lesson plans, taught the morals and significance of high school. Another one of my passions is technology. It was broaden, when my teacher advised I take the program ScriptEd coding class in my junior year. After just a few months of learning the basics of the computer languages of HTML,CSS and Javascript, it lead me to becoming a 2016 ScriptEd Hackathon 1st place winner, hosted at Google. I completed the scripted class, and was introduced to the girl who codes Summer Immersion program. I connected my passion of technology along with my newfound career path, Computer Science. Its exciting to participate because this an great opportunity to explore the technological field with my peers and do something I love, coding.
